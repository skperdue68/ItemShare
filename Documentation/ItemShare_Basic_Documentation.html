ItemShare - ESO Addon

Overview

ItemShare is an addon that helps you keep track of items you are willing to share with others.

On its own, the addon has a very limited role. It simply collects item data and stores it in your SavedVariables. That data is then used outside of the game in a Google Sheets document, where it becomes a full item-sharing system.

The spreadsheet allows you and others to:

  • See what items are available
  • Browse and search items easily
  • Mark items you would like to receive
  • Coordinate sharing without using guild banks or chat spam

Why Use ItemShare?

  • Avoid spamming guild chat with item lists
  • Share items without relying on guild bank access
  • Keep a clean, organized list of what you have available

Requirements

  • LibCustomMenu (required)
  • Elder Scrolls Online (current version)

Quick Start

  1. Right-click an item in your inventory and choose Add to Share
  2. Repeat for any items you want to share
  3. Run /ishare save or reload your UI
  4. Upload your SavedVariables file to the Google Sheet
  5. Use the spreadsheet to manage requests

What the Addon Does

  • Adds right-click options to inventory items so you can add or remove them from your shared list
  • Shows an icon for items that are marked as shared
  • Keeps track of item counts and where they are stored
  • Provides a window (/itemshare list) showing your shared items
  • Saves your data for use in Google Sheets
  • Allows you to store and share your spreadsheet link in chat

List Window

The Shared Items window shows everything currently marked for sharing.

  • Right-click an item to turn sharing off
  • Right-click again to turn sharing back on
  • Left-click the item name to view full item details

When you close and reopen the window, it will only show items that are currently being shared. This is expected behavior.

Commands

CommandDescription
/itemshareShows basic help
/itemshare listOpen or close the shared items window
/itemshare resetClear all shared items
/itemshare syncUpdate your list with items you still have
/itemshare saveReload UI to save your data
/itemshare debugToggle debug messages
/itemshare sheetView or set your spreadsheet link
/itemshare sheet <url>Save your spreadsheet link
/itemshare sheet clearClear the stored link
/itemshare sharelinkPlace your sheet link into chat

Alias: /ishare

Google Sheets Template & Setup

For your convenience, a Google Sheets template document is available:

Google Sheet Template

After opening the template, click Use Template and save a copy to your Google Drive.

Then update sharing settings by going to: File → Share → Share with others and setting it to Anyone with the link with Editor access.

Save the URL of your sheet. You can store it in-game using: /itemshare sheet <url>

Sample data is included in the SampleData folder if you want to see how everything works.

Notes

  • You must reload your UI to save data before exporting
  • The addon does not upload data automatically
  • All processing happens in Google Sheets

Known Issues

  • Marking items in a guild bank as shareable is currently disabled pending further testing
  • If an item is marked as shared while in the Furnishing Vault and is later placed in a house, it will continue to show as shared. This behavior may change in a future update
